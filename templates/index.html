Fettucine Alfredo

This Italian culinary dish brings back a wave of nostalgia from my childhood hometown Charleston, West Virginia. My family would always have Saturday evening dinners at two Italian restraunts called The Forum and Fazios. My parents always ordered for me the fettucine alfredo that came with either chicken, broccoli or shrimp. The past 14 years of my dining experince has been shaped by this creamy Italian dish in the crackhead valleys of Applaicha.
